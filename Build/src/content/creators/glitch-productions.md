**GLITCH Productions** is an independent animation studio founded by Kevin and Luke Lerdwichagul (SMG4) in 2019, specializing in high-quality serialized animated content on YouTube.

The studio has become one of the most successful independent animation companies, known for producing cinematic-quality shows with compelling narratives, complex characters, and stunning visuals. Their shows have collectively garnered hundreds of millions of views.

GLITCH is particularly notable for supporting new creators - both *Murder Drones* (created by Liam Vickers) and *The Amazing Digital Circus* (created by Gooseworx) were passion projects that GLITCH helped bring to life with full production support.

### Notable Shows

- **Meta Runner** (2019-2021) - Their first original series, a sci-fi story about a girl trapped in video games
- **Sunset Paradise** (2021) - A tropical adventure spin-off
- **Murder Drones** (2021-2024) - Sci-fi horror-comedy about killer robots on a frozen planet
- **The Amazing Digital Circus** (2023-present) - A psychological horror-comedy about characters trapped in a digital world
- **The Gaslight District** (2025-present) - A mystery noir series
- **Knights of Guinevere** (2025-present) - A psychological thriller set in the far future about a theme park and its mascot

### Studio Philosophy

GLITCH Productions focuses on:
- Supporting independent creators and their visions
- High-quality production values
- Serialized storytelling with continuity
- Building dedicated fan communities
- Maintaining creative freedom while on YouTube

### Impact

The studio has proven that independent animation can compete with traditional studios in terms of quality and audience engagement, paving the way for other indie creators.
