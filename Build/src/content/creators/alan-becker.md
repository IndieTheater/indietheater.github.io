**Alan Becker** is an animator and YouTuber best known for his *Animator vs. Animation* series, which began in 2006 and became one of the most iconic stick figure animation series on the internet.

His work combines creative storytelling with technical animation skills, featuring stick figures that interact with desktop environments, software applications, and video games. The *Animation vs. Minecraft* series has garnered hundreds of millions of views, showcasing incredible fight choreography and creative use of Minecraft mechanics.

### Notable Works
- **Animator vs. Animation** - The original series that launched his career
- **Animation vs. Minecraft** - Epic adventures in the Minecraft world
- **Animation vs. Everything** - Stick figures take on various challenges
- **Animation vs. Actual Shorts** - Standalone animated shorts
