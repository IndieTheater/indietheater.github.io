**SpindleHorse Toons** is an independent animation studio founded by Vivienne "VivziePop" Medrano, best known for creating the adult animated musical series *Hazbin Hotel* and its spin-off *Helluva Boss*.

The studio gained massive popularity through YouTube, with *Hazbin Hotel*'s pilot episode (2019) becoming a viral sensation. The success led to *Hazbin Hotel* being picked up by Amazon Prime Video for a full series in 2024, while *Helluva Boss* continues as a YouTube series.

SpindleHorse's work is characterized by vibrant art styles, complex characters, musical numbers, and mature themes set in Hell. The studio has built one of the largest followings in independent animation.

### Notable Works

- **Hazbin Hotel** - Musical comedy about a hotel for rehabilitating demons
- **Helluva Boss** - Dark comedy spin-off about assassins from Hell
