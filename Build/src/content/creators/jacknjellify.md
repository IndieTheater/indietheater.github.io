**JacknJellify** is an animation team created by Cary and Michael Huang, best known for creating *Battle for Dream Island* (BFDI), one of the longest-running and most influential object shows on the internet.
  
BFDI pioneered the object show genre when it launched in 2010, featuring anthropomorphic objects competing in challenges for a grand prize. The show's success spawned numerous seasons, spin-offs, and inspired countless similar shows across YouTube.
  
The series is known for its large cast of colorful characters, fan voting mechanics, and community engagement. BFDI has built one of the most dedicated fan communities in online animation, with millions of viewers following the show for over a decade.
  
## Notable Works
- **Battle for Dream Island (BFDI)** - The original groundbreaking object show
- **Battle for BFDI** - Continuation of the main series
- **BFDI Mini** - Shorter episodes and side content
  
## Community Impact
- Pioneered the object show genre
- One of the first shows to use viewer voting
- Massive fan community creating their own object shows
- Over 15 years of continuous content
